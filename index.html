Product Guide

New to plastic raw materials? Here's a simple guide to help you pick the right product for your needs.

🎞️

BOPP

Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene

Best for: Food packaging, tape manufacturing, lamination, labels, gift wrapping.

Features: Crystal clear, moisture resistant, strong, glossy finish.

⚙️

HP

High-Performance Plastic

Best for: Industrial parts, machinery components, tough applications.

Features: Durable, heat resistant, chemical resistant, long-lasting.

🛍️

LD

Low Density Polyethylene

Best for: Plastic bags, flexible films, squeeze bottles, food wraps.

Features: Flexible, lightweight, waterproof, easy to mold.

💪

Super LD

Superior-Grade LD

Best for: Heavy-duty packaging, industrial bags, protective covers.

Features: Extra strong, puncture resistant, premium quality.

🧱

BP

Bulk Plastic Granules

Best for: Mass manufacturing, molding, general plastic production.

Features: Cost-effective, versatile, available in bulk.

📦

Packaging Products

Bags, Rolls & Sheets

Best for: Retail packing, wholesale, shipping, storage.

Features: Ready-to-use, various sizes, custom options available.
